TwEpoch

The Twitter Trends Archive

About

TwEpoch is intended to keep a record of trends on Twitter.

We often see things that are popular on Twitter for a day or so, and then they are lost to time, so this website was intended to be a way to look back on certain days in history, and be able to pinpoint the conversations tha were happening on the most popular social media platform of the time.

The point of making the website is to have a way to organize and visualize the data I collect, and for others to more easily be able to see it as well.
